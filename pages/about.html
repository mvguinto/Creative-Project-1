About

Derek Hensley

Derek loves dogs, although that should be obvious from the picture. He is currently 24 and a student at Brigham Young University Ever since he was young he has loved playing video games. Starting with the Nintendo 64 that was bought by his brothers, he grew up up on such classics as Super Mario 64, Banjo-Kazooie, and the Legend of Zelda, both Ocarania of Time and Majora's Mask. Throughout the rest of his life he has enjoyed many other consoles such as Xbox, PlayStation, and the Wii. Today he enjoys playing on his Switch along with the occasional adventure onto PC, when the time permits. He is a physics major and when he grows up, he hopes to be able to work on computer networking.

Maleah Guinto

Maleah is a 24 year old student at Brigham Young University. Her interest in video games began when she was very young, and she credits this to her older brother and uncles. She currently owns a PlayStation 4, gaming PC, 3DS, Switch, and PSP. She's not a hardcore gamer and considers gaming to be therapeutic. Her favorite games to play are RPG's, though she's pretty open to any game if she's playing with family. She is studying Computer Science with an Emphasis in animation at BYU, and she hopes to create models and textures for video games upon graduating.
